About Us

With over 29 years of experience, Live Oak Dog Obedience is an established local business operating in LA and San Bernardio County since 1982. Live Oak Dog Obedience offers year-round dog training classes, and Private In Home Lessons, Trick Classes, in City of Rancho Cucamonga, City of Upland, City of Claremont and City of Glendora.  Classes are generally small so personalized attention and guidance can be achieved. We are proud to serve our new and continuing clients since 1982. Through non-abusive training techniques, we strive to achieve a well-mannered dog for your family to enjoy. Our trainer’s/instructors are committed to helping each and every client and their dog to their fullest extent with obedience control, behavior prevention & modification.

We continue to expand to more cities! Live Oak Dog Obedience is an establishment recommended by veterinarians and is proud of its outstanding reputation.
